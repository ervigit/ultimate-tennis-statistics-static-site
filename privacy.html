Ultimate Tennis Statistics

Privacy policy

The Ultimate Tennis Statistics iOS app developers do not collect any personally identifiable information about the users. This app sends anonymous usage information to the privacy-first analytics service TelemetryDeck. For more details, you can read their privacy policy.

This analytics data provide information how the app features are used. This anonymous information is used only by the developers to help improve the app experience. Apart from this anonymous analytics data, the developers do not collect, store, use or get access to any of your data.
